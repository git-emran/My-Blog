# Importance of Art in the Age of AI

What is art ? I often asked myself that question, when I was literally 7 years old. In my school, art was drawing and painting. But my friend showed me that it wasn't just limited to drawing and painting, art is actually how you express yourself.

#### Expressing yourself

We as a human have invented few different ways of expressing our thoughts. Obvious one is speaking, which is basically making sounds with mouth, we call it language.
